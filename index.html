Nice to meet ya!

I’ve been working as a freelance graphic designer for the last 7 years. In 2018, I decided to extend my skills to the world of development. I was fortunate to be selected to start a 3 month internship at 8th Light, where I learned the basics of back-end programming.

At the beginning of April, I met a few of the Founders & Coders apprentices at a Codebar meet-up. They introduced me to the Founders & Coders community and I really enjoy it. I want to be a part of the club!

The bootcamp angle of learning JavaScript makes complete sense in regards to my design background, and naturally I’ve decided to apply here.

The concept of learning from the previous cohort is fantastic. Pairing and mobbing with fellow apprentices is a great opportunity to learn from and teach others, as well as moving forward together.

PS: The people I’ve met from Founders & Coders are great, and the place is awesome too! I’m looking forward to hearing from you!
