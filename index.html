Since then, I want to be part of the club! In the beginning of April, I meet few of the apprentices of Founders & Coders in a workshop meeting at Codebar. The following Monday, they introduce me to the Founders & Coders community and I really enjoy it. The bootcamp angle of learning JavaScript make completely sense in regard of my design background and it’s naturally that I decided to apply here.

The concept of learning from the previous cohort is fantastic, as the pairing and mobbing the fellows apprentices to get better together.

PS: The people and the place are awesome! I’m looking forward to hear from you!
